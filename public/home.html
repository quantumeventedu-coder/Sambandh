Every feature, and exactly how it works

Nothing on this page is marketing fluff — each claim below is enforced by the product itself.

Verified humans only

Government ID via DigiLocker in ~30 seconds (or instant document check), plus a selfie liveness test that becomes your first profile photo — the face you see is the face on the ID. Doctors, lawyers, CAs & architects get registry-verified profession badges. Aadhaar numbers are never stored.

No free tier = no bots

Membership is monthly: CHF 1 men · CHF 5 women · CHF 3 non-binary. It's a bot barrier, not a business model — and it's fully refundable for 24 hours, no questions asked.

The Karma Book

An AI honesty engine scores every member 0–100. It reads what people claim and checks it against what they actually do. First slip: private warning. Repeat patterns: visible flags. Honesty heals — +1 karma per 30 clean days.

Evidence, with guard-rails

See a flag on a profile? Pay CHF 0.50–1 to read the anonymized statements behind it. The flagged person learns someone looked — never who. Capped at 3/day. Fraud alerts are always free.

Fair questions

FAQ

 

Does the AI read my chats?

AI analysis runs privately over your own messages to extract claims you make about yourself and conduct signals. No human reads your chats, and no chat content is ever shown to other users — only flag-level summaries (and anonymized excerpts behind a paid, rate-limited, notified reveal). Fraud detection protects everyone and is free to see.

What happens to my Aadhaar / documents?

Aadhaar numbers are never stored — DigiLocker verification keeps only a token reference. Uploaded document images are automatically deleted after 30 days. Document numbers are stored masked (XXXX-1234).

Why does the base fee differ by gender?

The monthly base fee is an anti-bot barrier calibrated to typical abuse patterns, not a revenue model. After joining, everyone is on the same footing: a daily free allowance (women, non-binary & others get the more generous 20 messages + 5 new chats/day; men 10 + 3), with the same optional Sambandh Pro (CHF 6/month, unlimited messaging) or Sambandh Max (CHF 15/month, everything) subscriptions.

Can I be flagged unfairly?

The AI is deliberately conservative (moderate-to-high confidence only), first offenses are private warnings, changes of heart over months are explicitly not contradictions, and every flag is disputable with score restoration if upheld.

Can I delete everything?

Yes — deletion erases your profile, karma, claims, likes, notifications and verifications within 30 days; your messages become "[deleted]" for the other person, and your phone number is freed for reuse. Export your data as JSON any time before that.
